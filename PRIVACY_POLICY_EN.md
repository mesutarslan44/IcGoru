# Privacy Policy - İçGörü: Pro

**Last Updated:** December 22, 2024

## Introduction

İçGörü: Pro application ("App") takes the privacy of its users very seriously. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled when you use the App.

## Data Collection

### Data Collected
İçGörü: Pro application **does not send any personal data to our servers**. All test results and data are stored locally on your device only.

The App stores the following data **only on your device**:
- Psychological test responses
- Test results and analyses
- Personal notes (optionally added by user)
- Historical test records

### Data NOT Collected
- Personal information such as name, email, or phone number
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage statistics

## Data Storage

All data is stored in your device's local storage (AsyncStorage). This data:
- Exists only on your device
- Is never sent to any server or third party
- Is completely deleted when you uninstall the app
- Can be manually deleted from within the app

## Third-Party Services

This app may use the following third-party services:
- **Expo (React Native):** Used for the application framework
- **Google Play Store:** Used for app distribution

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under 13 years of age.

## Security

Since your data is stored only on your device, security depends on your device's security. We recommend protecting your device with a password or biometric authentication.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be notified within the app.
